About me

I’m am an undergrad from BITS Pilani with major in EEE and a minor in Physics. Presently, for my undergraduate thesis I’m working on, combined approach for exploration and search of tough environments like mines and caves.

I’m currently interested in Robotics, particularly motion planning and perception. I was an active member of the Electronics and Robotics club on our campus during my undergraduate years, and I was also part of the autonomous mars rover team where I was responsible for developing the control and interfacing sub-system of the rover.

During breaks, I love to trek and to take pictures. I occasionally upload pictures on my Instagram. I like to read about astronomy & astrophysics and I am also into PC gaming.
